# Grid

<Figure caption={`Ant Design Grid Component`}>
  <Blockquote cite="https://ant.design/components/grid">
    <RichText Tag={`p`}>
      In most business situations, Ant Design needs to solve a lot of
      information storage problems within the design area, so based on 12 Grids
      System, we divided the design area into 24 sections.
    </RichText>
    <RichText Tag={`p`}>
      We name the divided area 'box'. We suggest four boxes for horizontal
      arrangement at most, one at least. Boxes are proportional to the entire
      screen as shown in the picture above. To ensure a high level of visual
      comfort, we customize the typography inside of the box based on the box
      unit.
    </RichText>
  </Blockquote>
</Figure>

For more information about the Ant Design Grid system & how to use it, see https://ant.design/components/grid.
